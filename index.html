Buying decisions are broken.

Search results are crowded. Reviews are often shallow. Influencer content is increasingly paid. And many genuinely good products from honest businesses remain invisible.

What Aava is building

Aava helps people share the real context behind a product: why they bought it, what problem it solved, what worked, what did not, who should buy it, and who should avoid it.
